How to Play :
Select a party color below, your goal is to rig a majority of the map in their favor!
• Drag to connect star tiles into voting districts - connect in any direction, but districts can’t cross
• Each district must contain the same number of stars (not counting gray), ties create split districts that can help either side
• Win a district by controlling more than half its stars, win the map by controlling more than half its districts
• Tap a district to remove it and try again
The daily map changes every day but you can play other challenge maps below or make your own!

What is Gerrymandering?
Gerrymandering is a political tactic where those in power redraw voting districts to keep themselves in control - manipulating the rules to silence opposition and cling to power. It lets politicians pick their voters, instead of voters picking their leaders. By slicing communities apart, they can rig outcomes even when they’re unpopular. This game puts you in their shoes. Can you win without winning fair?
